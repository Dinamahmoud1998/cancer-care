Who you are

Can care is an award-winning free mobile application from the Egyptian Society of Clinical Oncology. Available on both IOS and Android devices, Can Care provides a full collection of tools to help manage your cancer care – from diagnosis through treatment and beyond, including the latest oncologist-approved cancer information. Each section of the app is designed to offer high quality resources that are user-friendly for patients, caregivers, and loved ones You and your health care team will work together to plan your follow-up care. Your plan will be designed just for you, and it will guide your health care for the months and years after treatment. It will probably include regular physical examinations and medical tests. A follow-up care plan is often based on the medical guidelines for your specific type and stage of cancer. Your doctor will also keep your needs and wishes in mind when planning follow-up care. .

Medical consultation

You would get a referral to a medical oncologist, a physician who specializes in diagnosing and treating cancer, if your primary care physician suspects that your symptoms are suggestive of cancer. This is the essence of an oncology consultation.

Medical Reports

The pathology report provides the definitive cancer diagnosis. It is also used for staging (describing the extent of cancer within the body, especially whether it has spread) and to help plan treatment.
